Ping Pong

A quick and easy game to help you count with "ping" and "pong!"

~Ta Dah!~

    Let's get started!

    Rules:

    Pingpong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

    1. Numbers divisible by 3 become 'ping'
    2. Numbers divisible by 5 become 'pong'
    3. Numbers divisible by 15 become 'pingpong'
